Pigs

Pigs are incredibly intelligent animal. They can be trained better than most species of dogs.They enjoy playing games together and massage.

  • Scientific Name: pigs
  • Average Length: 0.9m-1.8m
  • Average Lifespan: 15-20years
  • Habitat: Wherever there is food
